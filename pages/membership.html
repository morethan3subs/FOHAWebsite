FALLING OAKS MEMBERSHIP INFORMATION

Falling Oaks offers a discount for memberships paid with cash or check. You may also pay via Square/Paypal. Memberships are available to Medina County Residents.

Two ways to pay

1. Pay with Cash or Check

  • Download the membership form here - FOHA MEMBERSHIP FORM 2025
  • Complete the form
  • Put the form and check in an envelope and put it in the Green Drop Box or mail it to 810 Guilford Blvd, Medina OH 44256

2. Pay with Credit Card/Square/Paypal

  • Download the membership form here - FOHA MEMBERSHIP FORM 2025
  • Complete the form
  • Put the form and check in an envelope and put it in the Green Drop Box or mail it to 810 Guilford Blvd, Medina OH 44256
  • Pay using our ONLINE MARKETPLACE
